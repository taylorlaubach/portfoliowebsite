About Me!

My passion for User Experience is driven by an innate curiosity about how people think and relate to the world around them. I love exploring how perception is shaped by the details we encounter and using this to craft smoother interactions and more intuitive tools.

I'm currently the UX Design lead at a company making enterprise-level database software, and I'm looking to take the next step in my career. To that end, I welcome any freelance or full-time opportunities.
